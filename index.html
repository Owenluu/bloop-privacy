bloop. Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bloop ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Bloop mobile application (the "App"). Bloop is operated by Owen Luu, based in Chicago, Illinois, USA.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Bloop, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you sign in (via Apple Sign-In or email).
  • Audio recordings: Audio you record using the App (such as sermons, meetings, conversations, reflections, or interviews).
  • Transcripts and summaries: Text generated from your recordings using third-party AI services.
  • Notes and flashcards: Any text content you create or save within the App.
  • Usage data: Basic technical information such as device type, operating system version, and crash reports to help us improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of the App: recording, transcribing, summarizing, and organizing your content.
  • To save your recordings and notes so you can access them across sessions.
  • To improve and maintain the App.
  • To respond to your support requests.

3. Third-Party Services

Bloop uses the following third-party services to provide its features. When you use Bloop, your data may be processed by these services subject to their own privacy policies:

  • OpenAI (Whisper): Audio recordings are sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude): Transcripts may be sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate summaries and identify key themes. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • API.Bible: Scripture references identified in your content may be looked up using API.Bible. See API.Bible's privacy terms.
  • Supabase: We use Supabase to securely store your account information and (in future versions) your recordings and notes. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Apple, Apple handles authentication. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your recordings are stored locally on your device by default. If you enable cloud sync (in future versions of the App), your recordings and notes will be stored on Supabase servers using industry-standard encryption. We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of the data we have about you.
  • Delete your data: Delete recordings, notes, or your entire account at any time from within the App or by contacting us.
  • Withdraw consent: Stop using the App and request deletion of your data.

6. Children's Privacy

Bloop is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
